Slovenia by Travel Blog

  1. Home
  2. Blog Post

Posted on August 17, 2022 at 4:42 UTC

This little nation on the western side of the Alps is home to a wide variety of attractions and activities, making it a good choice for vacationers. People in Slovenia are warm and kind, and the country has stunning natural scenery, delectable cuisine, and a significant cultural and historical legacy. It is an excellent place to go for either a short excursion of a single day or a longer vacation of up to a week.

Slovenia also has a lot to offer in terms of family and travel related travel destinations. It is home to a number of UNESCO World Heritage Sites, such as the 2,000-year-old Roman bridge of Stari Most, and the 14th-century castle at the top of the world in Ptuj. The country is also a hot spot for excellent skiing resorts, which come in the shape of Kranjska Gora and Jezersko. Visitors looking for a winter sports holiday can also enjoy skiing in and around Ljubljana and Triglav National Park.

In spite of these beauty and culture, Slovenians themselves are a friendly and unpretentious people.

Travelers can either explore the region by car or plane. The best way to travel is by road, as Slovenian roads are in top shape, even more than the European ones.

Slovenia is a part of both Europe and the United Nations, which contributes to the ease of movement of people.

Most of the accommodation options in the country are priced by budget, making it perfect for both budget travellers as well as those on a specific holiday budget.

While booking, it is also advisable to confirm whether there are any customs and immigration requirements as per your destination. Most tourists prefer to stay in a place for at least 2 nights to take advantage of local facilities such as museums, cafes and bars, while the rest of the day is devoted to exploring Slovenia.

The low-cost, yet high-quality accommodations in Slovenia are usually found in farmhouses and camping grounds. Accommodation options range from youth hostels and youth camps to dormitory facilities, hostels, hotels, bed and breakfasts, camping sites, and cottages.
