Port Saplaya, Valencia, Spain by Travel Blog

  1. Home
  2. Blog Post

Posted on August 17, 2022 at 4:42 UTC

Port Saplaya, which is located relatively near to the city of Valencia, is a very popular location for fishing. The beach is equipped with a wide variety of facilities, including ramps for people with disabilities and a boardwalk that leads from the sand to the water's edge. A sailing club and eateries may be found along the promenade that is there.

For your trip to Port Saplaya , consider these hotels

The Port Saplaya Hotel

If you're in the mood to spend the night in a luxurious hotel, this one is definitely a worthy choice. Here you will find all that you need for a memorable and relaxing vacation. With 101 rooms, including 28 rooms equipped with amenities, this hotel is ideal for both business travelers and leisure travelers. Guests can enjoy free WiFi, a large swimming pool with a slide, a fitness center and a free continental breakfast every morning. The complimentary car service is a nice added value. For details, visit https://www.port-saplaya.com/

The Port Saplaya Beach Hotel

With seven floors, this spacious hotel offers 152 standard rooms, with spacious bathrooms. The hotel is equipped with a bar and a caf√© and is strategically located a few minutes walk from the beach and shopping centers. Guests will have free Internet access. This hotel also has a pool, sauna, Jacuzzi and outdoor terraces. The WiFi is complimentary and so are many of the amenities. For more information, visit https://www.port-saplaya.com/

La Residencia

La Residencia is a romantic and luxurious hotel in Port Saplaya, that offers a comfortable and relaxing place to stay. The hotel offers a great variety of rooms, all of which have their own bathroom and balcony or terrace. The hotel is in the city's north, and just a short walk away from the beach and the city's various restaurants and bars. For more information, visit https://www.laresidencia.es/en.
